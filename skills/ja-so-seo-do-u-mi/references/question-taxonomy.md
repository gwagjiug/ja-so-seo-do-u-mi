# Question Taxonomy

Classify the 자기소개서 문항 and derive mandatory answer elements.

## Types

### 지원동기

Markers: 지원동기, 왜 지원, 관심을 갖게 된 계기, 우리 회사.

Required:

- job/company reason grounded in JD or product/domain
- candidate experience or interest that explains fit
- contribution direction

Avoid:

- company praise only
- "성장 가능성이 높아 지원했습니다"
- generic industry admiration

### 직무역량

Markers: 직무 수행, 역량, 강점, 전문성, 준비한 것.

Required:

- one relevant experience
- user's role
- action/process
- result/change/learning
- JD connection

Avoid:

- listing traits without proof
- unrelated awards or chronology

### 경험/성과

Markers: 성취, 성과, 가장 의미 있었던 경험, 프로젝트.

Required:

- challenge or goal
- user's specific contribution
- result
- reason this matters for the role

Avoid:

- long background
- team success without individual contribution

### 협업/갈등

Markers: 협업, 팀워크, 갈등, 소통, 조율.

Required:

- conflict or coordination problem
- what the user did
- how others responded or what changed
- collaboration principle learned

Avoid:

- blaming others
- "대화로 해결했습니다" with no method

### 실패/극복

Markers: 실패, 어려움, 위기, 극복, 도전.

Required:

- failure or obstacle
- cause analysis
- changed action
- result or learning

Avoid:

- fake weakness
- dramatic emotion without improved behavior

### 성격 장단점

Markers: 장점, 단점, 성격, 보완점.

Required:

- job-relevant strength with evidence
- non-fatal weakness
- concrete mitigation behavior

Avoid:

- "완벽주의" as a disguised strength unless concretely handled
- weaknesses that directly break the role

### 성장과정/가치관

Markers: 성장과정, 가치관, 인생관, 영향을 준 경험.

Required:

- decisive event, not life chronology
- value formed
- how it appears in work behavior

Avoid:

- childhood chronology
- family story with no job connection

### 입사 후 포부

Markers: 입사 후, 포부, 계획, 기여, 목표.

Required:

- first contribution area based on responsibilities
- realistic early actions
- medium-term growth direction only if supported

Avoid:

- "전문가로 성장"
- "회사와 함께 성장"
- grand visions with no job action

### 자유문항

Markers: 자유롭게, 본인을 소개, 기타.

Required:

- choose the strongest role-relevant evidence
- clear one-line positioning
- concise proof

Avoid:

- covering every life story
- generic personality introduction
