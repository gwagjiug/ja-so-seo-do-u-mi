# Diagnosis Taxonomy

Use these categories to detect why a rough 자기소개서 draft is weak.

## D-K. Cliche

Signals:

- 귀사에 기여하겠습니다
- 끊임없이 성장하겠습니다
- 최선을 다하겠습니다
- 빠르게 적응하겠습니다
- 소통을 중시합니다

Fix: replace with JD-specific action or delete.

## D-L. Unsupported Trait

Signals:

- 책임감, 소통능력, 문제해결력, 리더십, 주인의식, 분석력
- no concrete behavior nearby

Fix: convert trait into action and result.

## D-M. Weak Experience Structure

Signals:

- situation and emotion dominate
- user's action is one sentence or absent
- result is missing
- lesson is generic

Fix: compress background, expand action/process/result.

## D-N. JD Mismatch

Signals:

- experience is unrelated to responsibilities
- answer repeats company values but not job work
- prompt asks one thing and draft answers another

Fix: choose a relevant evidence angle from the draft and connect it to responsibilities or requirements.

## D-O. Fabrication Risk

Signals:

- draft says "주도" but role is unclear
- new number would make the sentence stronger but is absent
- tool/skill appears in JD but not in draft
- team achievement may be overstated as personal achievement

Fix: lower certainty, preserve ambiguity, or ask the user.

## D-P. Submission Risk

Signals:

- over/under length
- wrong company or role
- blind-hiring prohibited identity markers
- unexplained abbreviation
- typo or broken sentence

Fix: audit before final output.

## D-Q. Reader Burden

Signals:

- important point appears after half the essay
- too many experiences in one answer
- no clear one-line thesis

Fix: start with the strongest answer and use one main experience.

## D-R. JD Overfit

Signals:

- many JD keywords appear, but the applicant's actual situation and action are thin
- tools, methods, or slogans become the main subject of the answer
- the essay sounds like "I will use X to improve Y" without showing what problem, target, constraint, or output the applicant understands
- preferred qualifications are repeated more than responsibilities

Fix: remove low-signal JD terms, keep one or two strong JD links, and return the paragraph to the applicant's real evidence.

## D-S. Abstract Promise

Signals:

- paragraph ends with `개선하겠습니다`, `기여하겠습니다`, or `성장하겠습니다` without a concrete first action
- future plan is broad enough to fit any company or role
- no role-neutral anchor appears nearby, such as stakeholder, problem, work context, decision, deliverable, process, result, or constraint

Fix: replace the promise with a realistic next action based on the JD and draft. Use role-specific concreteness without forcing one occupation's examples.
